concat-methods {NeuroImage}R Documentation

concatenate two image objects

Description

concatenate two image objects in the nth dimension (usualy time)

Methods

x = "BrainVector", y = "BrainVector"
concatenates two BrainVectors in the fourth dimension
x = "BrainVector", y = "BrainVolume"
concatenates a BrainVector and a BrainVolume in the fourth dimension
x = "BrainVolume", y = "BrainVolume"
concatenates two BrainVolumes, to form a BrainVector
x = "SparseBrainVector", y = "SparseBrainVector"
concatenates two SparseBrainVectors
